Foam-Free Ready.

Illinois is moving away from foam. Whatever the timeline turns out to be, the products that replace it are already on our shelves. Switch on your schedule, not in a panic.

SB 0058 is live Since January 1, 2026, state agencies and their vendors can no longer buy foam foodware.
SB 1531 passed the Senate The retail foam ban is not law yet, but it is moving. Smart kitchens are switching early.
We stock the replacements Fabri-Kal and CKF fiber lines, Inline rPET clear containers. In our warehouse today.
